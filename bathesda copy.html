Bishop Johnson Ajemoh

Johnson Ejomah (Born January 12, 1953) is a Nigerian American minister and a Pentecostal Bishop.He is the senior pastor of Bethesda Community Church Int’l Incorporated located in the city of Antioch, California United States of America. Bishop Ejomah is one of ten children born to Mr. Oghenerurie and Mrs. Ajiro Ejomah. Since his parents divorced when he was a very young man, he was raised by his uncle Mr. J. J.
